Go ask the Service Desk!

aka Go Fish

"Go ask the Service Desk” is best for 3-6 players, but it is possible for 2 to play. The card deck is collection of UNUM applications that you must try and match to earn points. The dealer deals 5 cards to each player (7 each for 2 players). The remaining cards are placed face down to form a pile.

The player to dealer's left starts. A turn consists of asking a specific player for a specific UNUM application. For example, if it is my turn I might say: 'Heather, do you have any MERLIN’'. The player who asks must already hold at least one card of the requested app cards, so I must hold at least one MERLIN to say this. If the player who was asked (Heather) has cards of the named app (MERLIN in this case), she must give her card of this app to the player who asked for it. That player then gets another turn and may again ask any player for any app already held by the asker.

If the person asked does not have any cards of the named app, they say 'Go Ask the Service desk!'. The asker must then draw the top card of the undealt cards. If the drawn card is the app asked for, the asker shows it and gets another turn. If the drawn card is not the rank asked for, the asker keeps it, but the turn now passes to the next player to the left.

When a match is made the player puts the matched cards face down on the table. The game continues until either someone has no cards left in their hand or the cards run out. The winner is the player who then has the most sets of applications.
